# To-Do-List
Most to-do lists just let you write things down. I built this lightweight, browser-based app to actually help you get them done.

Built entirely with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, this tracker takes your daily tasks and automatically organizes them by their deadlines. To keep things visual, it color-codes your list based on urgency: tasks due today highlight in red, tasks due this week in yellow, and tasks due this month in green.

What I built in:

Smart Sorting: Tasks are automatically ordered by date, bubbling the most urgent items to the top.

Visual Urgency: Custom color-coding (Red/Yellow/Green) based on real-time date math.

Persistent Memory: Powered by browser Local Storage, meaning you never lose your list when you refresh or close the page—no database required.

Clean UI: A distraction-free, responsive design that makes adding and completing tasks feel satisfying.
